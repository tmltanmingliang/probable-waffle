Flappy Bird

Tap to fly and navigate through the pipes in this addictive arcade classic!

How to Play

  1. Tap the screen or click your mouse to make the bird fly up.
  2. Release to let the bird fall.
  3. Navigate through the pipes without touching them.
  4. Each pipe you pass through adds to your score.
  5. Beat your high score and share it with friends!

Game Features

  • Simple yet challenging gameplay
  • Classic arcade style graphics
  • Easy to learn, hard to master mechanics
  • Competitive high score system
  • Perfect for quick gaming sessions

Tips for Success

  • Tap with a consistent rhythm
  • Focus on the center of the pipes
  • Practice timing your taps
  • Keep a steady pace
  • Learn from each failed attempt

Game History

Flappy Bird is a popular mobile game that gained worldwide attention for its simple yet challenging gameplay. The game requires precise timing and quick reflexes as players navigate through increasingly difficult pipe obstacles.
